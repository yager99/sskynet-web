INTRODUCTION

　　SuperSkynet is creating an unprecedented network ecosystem by effectively combining advanced technologies such as Blockchain, AI, Internet of Things and Fog Computing. After years of development, Blockchain, AI, Internet of things, Fog Computing are now rebuilding the world. With the mechanism of mutual benefit for everything connected to it, SuperSkynet makes the simple information link between person and person a comprehensive network including links between person and object, object and object. Integrating the computing power of each node makes the super AI possible to serve human beings. We believe that SuperSkynet represents a new phase of human technology, and promotes a new model of global integration and efficient sharing. We believe it will make the world a better place.
