INTRODUCTION

　　SuperSkynet is creating an unprecedented network ecosystems by effectively combining advanced technologies such as blockchain, AI, Internet of Things and Fog Computing. After years of development, Blockchain, AI, Internet of things, Fog Computing are now rebuilding the world. With the mechanism of mutual benefit for everything connected into it, SuperSkynet makes the information from the simple link between person and person, to the links between person and object, object and object. Integrating the compute power of each node makes the super AI possible to serve the human beings. We believe that SuperSkynet represents a new phase of human technology, and breeds a new model of global integration and efficient sharing. We believe it will make the world a better place.
